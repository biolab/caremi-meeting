Background

The closing meeting of the CARE-MI EU FP7 project will be held in Ljubljana on Thursday, September 17, and Friday, September 18, 2015. The meeting will feature a summary of work done in CARE-MI workpackages, presentation of student posters, an open session with a set of CARE-MI and invited lectures and a wrap-up by leaders of the CARE-MI participants.

Local organisation:

The meeting is locally organized by Jerca Tekavec, Jasna Bevk and Blaž Zupan. Please contact us at srd (at) fri (dot) uni-lj (dot) si for questions regarding accomodation, transport or organisation.

Venue

The meeting will take place at the Faculty of Computer and Information Science, University of Ljubljana, Večna pot 113, 1000 Ljubljana, Slovenia.

Accommodation

Ljubljana is one of the smallest European capitals and there are plenty hotels close to the center of the city that are also relatively close to the workshop venue. You will though need to use either a car, city public transport, or a taxi to reach the venue.

In case you need any advice regarding the accommodation, please contact Uroš Petrovič.

Posters

We encourage attending students and post-docs to prepare posters describing their own research work that is related to the topics of the meeting. All poster presenters will recive a special participation award from the local organiser.
